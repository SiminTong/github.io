Master Student at Leiden Observatory

Hi, welcome to my homepage! I am Simin Tong (click for pronounciation, written as 思敏 童 in Chinese characters), a second-year master student at Leiden Observatory. Before moving to Leiden, I obtained my Bachelor's degrees from Jilin University (China), where I studied physics and law.

I am interested in a variety of topics on planetary systems, such as protoplanetary disks, planet formation and star formation.

My first master project is about characterizing the chemical composition of a high-mass star under the supervision of Prof. (Michiel) Hogerheijde , Dr. (Aida) Ahmadi and Prof. (Serena) Viti . We use the ALMA archival data as well as CASSIS to identify species and model spectra. This project will be continued in my second year.
